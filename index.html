About

Hi, my name is Nurul Najwa Binti Piher. I am 20 years old and I am from Machang, Kelantan.

I am a diploma student at UiTM Kedah, majoring in library informatics and currently in semester 4.
There is only 1 semester left for me to finish this diploma. I like to visit the library and research the reading materials there. I hope that everything will be made easier so that I and my friends can graduate successfully.

I don't know much about the jobs that I can apply for with the major I studied but if I am given the opportunity I want to be a library staff in an organization. This is because I am interested in the field of work of a library staff. I know it is not as easy as it looks but I would like to try it and maybe it is the dream job that I have dreamed of before. Pray for me so that I can achieve those goals and dreams.
